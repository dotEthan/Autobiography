About Me
My name is Ethan Strauss, I'm a passionate and endlessly curious front end developer. Over the last two decades I have traveled two continents using my understanding of HTML, CSS, Javascript and more to design and implement solutions to the problems I came across.


I have now settled in Toronto and reinvigorated my programming learning by diving into Node.js, React and more. I'd love to find a company where I can both contribute to solving any problems they have while also working and learning with people as dedicated and passionate as me.
